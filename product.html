About the product

With products like tinctures packaged in bottles that are susceptible to break, it is really important for the brands to choose a material for the boxes that protect the product housed in the box. There might be a risk of the tinctures getting infused with pollutants that cause their quality to drop significantly. No brand would be willing to take this risk. Thus, we offer customized boxes for tinctures that are made of cardboard material which is very sturdy and has different densities. The thickness of the boxes can be customized as well from anywhere to 12pt to 28pt. These super sturdy packaging boxes ensure that the product they are holding would reach their end customer with no breakage issues.
